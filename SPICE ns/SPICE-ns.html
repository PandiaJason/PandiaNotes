What is S P I C E ?

S P I C E is a Space Geometrical data & toolkit containing Spacecraft, Planet, Instrument, C-matrix, Events (S P I C E). It is developed by NAIF at NASA in the US. It is the interspace standard for Space Research and Mathematical Observations.
