What is S P I C E ?

S P I C E is a Space Geometrical data toolkit containing Spacecraft, Planet, Instrument, C-matrix, Events (S P I C E). It is developed by NAIF at NASA in the US. It is the interspace standard for Space Research and Mathematical Observations. S P I C E use cases extend from mission concept development through post-mission data analysis. It involves correlating individual instrument datasets from other instruments on the same or different spacecraft information.

S P I C E Kernels

S P I C E datasets are termed S P I C E kernels/ kernel files, with kernel files composed of astrodynamics, ancillary, and navigation data, providing precise observation geometry for space research and studies.
